About Us - Skylor's Fried Chicken

Welcome to Skylor's Fried Chicken, where we serve the crispiest and juiciest fried chicken in town! Founded in 1843, our mission has always been to provide delicious and affordable meals to our customers, using only the finest ingredients and time-tested recipes.

Over the years, we've grown from a humble neighborhood eatery to a beloved institution, serving our signature chicken dishes to families and friends across the city. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction remains unwavering, as we continue to innovate and expand our menu to cater to diverse tastes and preferences.

At Skylor's Fried Chicken, we take pride in our heritage and the community we serve. Whether you're craving classic fried chicken, savory sides, or refreshing beverages, we invite you to join us and experience the irresistible flavors that have made us a local favorite for generations.
